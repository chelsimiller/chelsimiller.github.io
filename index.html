About

I'm a first-year PhD student in the Learning Sciences program at Northwestern University. My research sits at the intersection of music/arts education, computer science education, human-computer interaction, and design-based research.

I am currently open to collaborative research projects regarding collaboration and digital-learning in STEAM environments.
