About

I'm a third-year PhD student in the Learning Sciences program at the Northwestern University, advised by Prof. Sarah Chen. My research sits at the intersection of educational psychology, human-computer interaction, and data-informed pedagogy.

Before my doctorate, I worked as a curriculum designer at a nonprofit serving underrepresented students in STEM, which continues to shape my commitment to research that translates into practice.
